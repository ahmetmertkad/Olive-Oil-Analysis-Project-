OBJECTIVE OF OUR PROJECT

Our project aims to raise awareness about the quality and consumption of olive oil, which is widely used in daily life. By providing critical parameters to evaluate the chemical, physical and sensory quality of olive oil, it helps consumers make more informed choices. This approach promotes a healthy lifestyle by highlighting the health benefits of olive oil and setting quality standards.

WHY IS QUALITY ASSESSMENT IMPORTANT?

Assessing the quality of olive oil is crucial for several reasons. Firstly, it ensures consumer satisfaction and trust by guaranteeing that the product meets specific standards of taste, aroma, and health benefits. Secondly, it protects against fraudulent practices such as adulteration or mislabeling, preserving the integrity of the industry. Additionally, accurate quality assessment helps producers maintain consistency in their products, fostering their reputation in the market and facilitating fair competition. Ultimately, by upholding quality standards, olive oil assessment promotes both consumer well-being and the sustainability of the olive oil industry..
